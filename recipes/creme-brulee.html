Creme Brulee

Creme that happens to be brulee'd

Ingredients
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 5 egg yolks/li>
  • 21/2 cup sugar

Let's get started

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees. In a saucepan, combine cream and salt. cook over low heat. Let sit for a few minutes, add vanilla extract.
  2. In a bowl, beat yolks and sugar together until light. Stir 1/4 of the cream into mixture. Pour sugar-egg mix into cream and stir. Pour into four 6-ounce ramekins and Place them in a baking dish; fill dish with boiling water halfway. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until centers are barely set. Cool completely. Refrigerate for several hours and up to a couple of days.
  3. when ready to serve, top each custard with about a teaspoon of sugar in a thin layer. Place ramekins in a broiler 2-3 inches from heat. Turn on broiler. Cook until sugar melts and browns, about 5 minutes. serve within 2 hours.
